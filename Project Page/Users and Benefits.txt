Users:

Members of the public who:
 - want to know what art is close by and available to visit.
 - visit the art and would like to make a comment about the art (mobile app linking to ACT Gov Website via moderator).

Artists who want to study existing art (to assist in producing a proposal)

ACT Gov who fund the art proposals 
 - Where to put current funds
 - What impact has past expenditure been (compare with other "goodness" data)
 - What projections should be in place for new developments, suburbs, and refresh of existing

Benefits:

Using technology to bring together urban planning, artists, and the public to improve the social(?) and cultural well being of the ACT community.
